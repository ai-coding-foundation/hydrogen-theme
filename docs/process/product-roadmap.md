# Product Roadmap

## Purpose

Phased delivery plan for the EVOHERA store, from MVP launch through 18-month expansion.

---

## Phased Delivery Overview

| Phase  | Timeline    | Deliverables                                                                                                                                                                                            | Success Criteria                                                              |
| ------ | ----------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **V1** | Weeks 0–10  | 3 core SKUs with 2 flavors each; Quiz → result page → recommended product + subscription default; Ingredient glossary MVP (search + filter by formula); 10 "Research Hub" articles (stage-based topics) | MVP live; quiz conversion tracked; glossary searchable; 10 articles published |
| **V2** | Months 3–6  | Refill economy (refill pouches for margin + sustainability); Membership layer ("Ask an expert" monthly Q&A + private community); Bundles ("90-day reset kit" 3 tins + "Flavor Variety Pack")            | Refill attach rate measured; membership sign-ups; bundle adoption             |
| **V3** | Months 6–18 | One adjacent category (e.g., "Sleep Support Night Mix" as single add-on); Retail readiness (store locator, wholesale portal, sampling program)                                                          | Adjacent SKU launched; retail partners onboarded; sampling program active     |
